FEATURES

Everything your child needs to love math

📷

Camera Finger Detection

Just hold up your fingers — the app reads them in real time using AI, no buttons needed!

🧮

Calculator Mode

Show two numbers with your fingers, pick + − × ÷, and see the result with a voice explanation.

🌟

Quiz Mode

The app shows a question — your child answers by holding up the right number of fingers.

🔊

Voice Read-Aloud

Every number and result is spoken aloud, making it perfect for early readers and non-readers.

✖️

All 4 Operations

Addition, subtraction, multiplication and division — with visual explanations like 2×3 = 2+2+2.

🔒

Safe for Kids

No ads, no in-app purchases, no data sharing. Camera is used only on-device — never stored or uploaded.

HOW IT WORKS

Three easy steps

1

Open the app

Allow camera access once — the app remembers your permission forever.

2

Hold up fingers

Show your fingers to the front camera. The AI counts them instantly.

3

Learn & celebrate

The app speaks the result, shows how it was calculated, and celebrates with your child!
